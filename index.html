Game Rules

Learn how to play Secret Hitler

Game Overview

Secret Hitler is a social deduction game set in 1930s Germany. Players are secretly divided into two teams: Liberals and Fascists. The Fascists know each other's identities, while the Liberals only know their own.

The goal is to either pass 5 Liberal policies (Liberal victory) or 6 Fascist policies (Fascist victory). Hitler's identity is hidden among the Fascists.

Game Setup

For 5-6 players: 3 Liberals, 2 Fascists (1 is Hitler)

For 7-8 players: 4 Liberals, 3 Fascists (1 is Hitler)

For 9-10 players: 5 Liberals, 4 Fascists (1 is Hitler)

Gameplay

Each round consists of:

  1. Election of a President and Chancellor
  2. Policy drafting and voting
  3. Special powers activation (if applicable)

Winning Conditions

Liberal Victory: Pass 5 Liberal policies

Fascist Victory: Pass 6 Fascist policies OR have Hitler elected Chancellor after 3 Fascist policies

Built for offline play - no accounts or servers required
