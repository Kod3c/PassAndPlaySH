Game Rules

Objective

Liberals must pass 5 Liberal policies to win. Fascists must either pass 6 Fascist policies or have Hitler elected Chancellor.

Game Flow

  1. Players are secretly assigned roles (Liberal, Fascist, or Hitler)
  2. Each round, a President is elected who nominates a Chancellor
  3. Players vote on the Chancellor
  4. If elected, the President and Chancellor draw and play policy cards
  5. Special powers are activated based on Fascist policies passed

Roles

  • Liberals: Must work together to pass Liberal policies
  • Fascists: Must deceive others and pass Fascist policies
  • Hitler: Must avoid being elected Chancellor while helping Fascists
