When can Fascists see comrades?

5-6 players: All Fascists (including Hitler) know each other and can see comrades.

7-10 players: Fascists know each other and can see comrades, but Hitler doesn't know who the other Fascists are.

This creates different strategic dynamics - in smaller games, Fascists can coordinate better, while in larger games, Hitler remains hidden.
