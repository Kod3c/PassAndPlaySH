Secret Hitler Rules

Game Overview

Secret Hitler is a social deduction game for 5-10 players. Players are secretly divided into two teams: Liberals and Fascists. The Fascists know each other's identities, while the Liberals only know their own. Hitler is hidden among the Fascists.

Objective

  • Liberals win by enacting 5 Liberal policies OR by identifying and executing Hitler
  • Fascists win by enacting 6 Fascist policies OR by having Hitler elected Chancellor after 3 Fascist policies are enacted

Game Flow

  1. Setup: Roles are secretly assigned to players
  2. Election: Players nominate and vote for President and Chancellor
  3. Legislation: President draws 3 policies, discards 1, Chancellor chooses from remaining 2
  4. Executive Powers: Certain Fascist policies unlock special powers
  5. Repeat: Continue until win condition is met

Executive Powers

  • 3rd Fascist Policy: Investigate Loyalty - President learns one player's role
  • 4th Fascist Policy: Policy Peek - President sees top 3 policies
  • 5th Fascist Policy: Special Election - President chooses next President
  • 6th Fascist Policy: Execution - President kills one player

Veto Power

When 5 Fascist policies are enacted, the Chancellor can propose a veto. If the President agrees, no policy is enacted and the election tracker advances.
